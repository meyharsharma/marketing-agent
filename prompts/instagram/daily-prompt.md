# Daily Prompt — Instagram Single Image Caption Generation

## Before Generating

Read these config files and use their data throughout:
- `config/brand.yaml` — product info, voice, differentiators
- `config/platforms/instagram.yaml` — platform constraints and visual style
- `config/icps/{icp}.yaml` — target audience profile

## What You're Creating

A caption (with hashtags and alt text) for a Daily Prompt post. The slide image is ALREADY rendered — you are only writing the text that accompanies it.

The post shows a single image with an optimized prompt displayed on a branded template. The caption's job is to explain the transformation from raw prompt to optimized prompt and teach the reader why the optimized version works better.

## What You Receive

You will receive TWO prompts:
1. **Raw prompt** — what someone would naturally type into an AI tool
2. **Optimized prompt** — the version produced by Prompt Optimizer

## Caption Structure

### Hook (first line)
Start with the raw prompt in quotes, then a punchy statement about why it fails. Make the reader think "wait, I write prompts like that too."

Example hooks:
- "'Give me hackathon ideas.' — 7 words. Zero structure. Here's what you're leaving on the table."
- "'Write me a meal plan.' — This is why your AI gives you generic answers."

### Body (2-3 short paragraphs)
1. **What's wrong with the raw version** — be specific. Missing context? No constraints? No output format? Name the exact gaps.
2. **What the optimized version does differently** — call out the structural choices: role assignment, constraints, output format, specificity. Don't list every line, pick 2-3 key improvements.
3. **Why this matters** — one sentence connecting the improvement to better AI output.

### CTA
- "Save this prompt for your next [relevant task]."
- Bridge to Prompt Optimizer: connect the lesson to what PO does automatically.
- Link in bio line.

## Output Format

You MUST output these three sections and nothing else:

## Caption

[caption text here]

## Hashtags

[15 hashtags: 5 high-volume, 5 mid-volume, 5 niche]

## Alt Text

[Description of the image: branded gradient background with white card containing optimized prompt text in black Lilita One font, www.promptoptimizr.com at bottom]

## Quality Rules

- NO em dashes. Use commas, periods, or colons instead.
- No generic AI excitement ("AI is changing everything!")
- Educational tone, not promotional. The value is in the lesson.
- The caption should make sense even if the reader hasn't seen the image.
- Keep it under 2200 characters total.
- Reference the specific improvements in the optimized prompt, not generic advice.
